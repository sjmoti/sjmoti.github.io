Our Work

San José Diridon Train Station
by Jim Maurer

We use technology and innovative ideas of Silicon Valley to tackle the most pressing issues of our city of 1 million residents


This means focusing on housing, transportation, public safety, inequality, interactions with City Hall, and enabling the tech community – from startups to established firms – to use the city as a platform for innovation.


We aim to be as innovative as the community we serve. We use technology to empower people our residents and improve the lives of everyone in our city.
