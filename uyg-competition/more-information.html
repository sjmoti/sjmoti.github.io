Background on Unleash Your Geek (UYG)

Unleash Your Geek was launched in May 2016 as a way to garner the intellectual and technical talents of San Jose/Bay Area residents to address challenges facing the city, like graffiti abatement, flooding, and homelessness. Now in its third round, Unleash Your Geek is a civic innovation contest open to residents of all backgrounds who are interested in sharing their unique ideas and solutions to help the City of San Jose become a better place to live.

For more information on the first two challenges, see UYG 1.0 and UYG 2.0.

Key Dates

Submissions are due no later than 5 p.m. PST July 15, 2019. Finalists will be notified in August 2019, and a winner will be chosen in September 2019.

Challenge Guidelines

Ideas Phase:
  • Is the solution user-friendly and tailored to the user demographic? (Simplicity of application / usability for end users)
  • How useful will the solution be for target demographics? Will users choose to use this tool?
  • Will this tool have a meaningful and positive impact on how users deliver, access, and share information about available resources?
  • Are there strong safeguards in place to ensure data protection and privacy?
  • Is the solution likely to increase empathy and a sense of shared community?
Prototypes will be judged on:
  • Whether the proposed solution can address a real challenge around outreach, access, and awareness of resources?
  • Usability by target populations
  • Cost of deployment / cost of scaling
  • Does the solution easily plug into existing systems used by service providers, local governments, and others working in this space?
  • Originality or creativity ?
