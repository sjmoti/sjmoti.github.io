Deploying the largest 5G small cell network in the US
BROADBAND & DIGITAL DIVIDE

In 2018, the City Council unanimously approved deals with three of the largest telecom firms to deploy 4,000+ 5G small cells across the city – the largest such deployment in the country. Proceeds from this deal will be used to close the digital divide in San José through a $20M+ Digital Inclusion Fund as well as streamlining the permitting process to speed deployment of broadband. These deals have been showcased as a national model by FCC Commissioner Rosenworcel as well as by U.S. Conference of Mayors and the National League of Cities.

What makes these deals special?

  • Largest 5G small cell deployment in the country, including $500M of private investment into the city in broadband
  • Improvement of 10-100X improvement in speeds download speeds for our residents
  • Market-based pricing for use of city infrastructure that fairly compensates taxpayers for the use of assets they paid for
  • Investment in building the capacity of the city team to move at the speed of business
  • Aesthetically pleasing design standards
  • Allocation of remaining proceeds from these agreement close the digital divide in San José
