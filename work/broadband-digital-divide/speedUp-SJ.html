SpeedUp San José

Crowdsourcing internet speeds across the city
BROADBAND & DIGITAL DIVIDE DATA

We built SpeedUp San José to crowdsource internet speeds across San José so we could have a better understanding of the availability of high speed internet service in our community. Over 3,000 San José residents participated by testing their internet speeds. Unfortunately the national statistics on broadband speeds published by the FCC are not accurate, so this tool helped us to take a data-driven approach to informing our city’s broadband strategy.

From the results, we learned that we often have slower speeds and lower broadband quality than what our residents deserve. We also were able to develop an understanding of neighborhoods where coverage gaps exist and where we should encourage investment. These results ultimately helped guide and justify our ambitious broadband strategy.

View the test results over on the project site: SpeedUp San José Results.
