Our Work Influencing Policy

Influencing Policy

Technology has become pervasive in our lives, so there’s more technology policy work than ever to be done. As expected, we work on city policy, but as the largest city in Silicon Valley, San José also has taken a leadership role in several state and federal level technology policy issues.

Equitable Broadband Deployment

Mayor Liccardo and our team inform state and national policy on broadband to ensure we aren’t leaving anyone on the wrong side of the digital divide.

Fighting California State Bill 649

California State Bill 649 would have excluded the public voice in the deployment of next generation broadband. We fought to get the bill vetoed so a more equitable one could be written.
