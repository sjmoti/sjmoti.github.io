Transportation Innovation Zone: a platform for testing mobility technologies

Giving innovators a platform to test their technology across a multi-mile network of streets, signals, and city infrastructure
TRANSPORTATION

In 2014, the City of San José created a “Transportation Innovation Zone” (TIZ) located in North San José that offers a multi-mile network of streets, signals, and infrastructure as a platform for technology innovators. With 21 traffic signals, over 670 streetlights and standard public transit infrastructure – this zone serves as a laboratory to develop tomorrow’s self-driving vehicles, automated traffic enforcement systems, vehicle to infrastructure tools, streetlight innovations, traffic sensors and more.

The TIZ has attracted partners testing new connected infrastructure and IoT applications. Initiatives like this moves us towards our goal of becoming the premier global hub of urban technology innovation for smart cities.
