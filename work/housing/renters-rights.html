Our Work Housing Renters' Rights App

Renters' Rights App

Working with local civic-minded hackers to develop a registry of renters' rights for San José residents.
HOUSING

What is the problem we are solving?

Many San José residents rent, but oftentimes they don’t know their rights when facing challenges around renting properties.

What did we do?

Code for San José is a group of volunteer technologists in the area who care about writing software that benefits their community. One of their teams recently created a Renter’s Rights web app to inform renters about laws and programs in place to protect them. For example, someone getting evicted could go on the the app to find simple language to learn their rights.

We love initiatives like this because they’re about residents helping other residents with technology. The Mayor’s Office of Technology and Innovation supported the volunteers with advice on user testing and how to get the word out and provided a small grant to help them run usability tests.

Check out the renters rights app at rentersrightsguide.org.

Thanks to Code for San José for using their tech skills for good!
