Data

We know that with better data, more access to that data, and more analytical capacity, we can serve our residents better. We’re committed to bringing more resources to bolster our data efforts.

Prioritizing housing inspections with big data

We worked with the University of Chicago to create algorithms to prioritize which multiple housing units our inspectors should visit first. This effort made us significantly better finding serious violations and will keep our population safer.

SpeedUp San José

We built SpeedUp San José to crowdsource internet speeds across the city to help us better understand the availability of high speed internet service in our community. Over 3,000 San José residents participated by testing their internet speeds.

Vision Zero

The San José joined a growing list of US cities implementing a Vision Zero initiative, which aims to reduce traffic fatalities to zero through education, enforcement and engineering in 2015. Since then, traffic fatalities have reduced from a peak of 60 in 2015 to 46 in 2017.
