Hi, we're the San José Mayor's Office of Technology & Innovation

We use technology and innovative ideas of Silicon Valley to tackle the most pressing issues of our city of 1 million residents.

This means focusing on housing, transportation, public safety, inequality, interactions with City Hall, and enabling the tech community – from startups to established firms – to use the city as a platform for innovation. However, we know we can't do this alone.

Thankfully, we can engage with our amazing innovative community!
