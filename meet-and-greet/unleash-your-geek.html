Unleash Your Geek

The “Unleash Your Geek” series of challenges brings together public and private resources to develop innovative solutions to some of the city’s biggest challenges. The first challenge, centered on graffitti abatement, was launched in 2016, followed by a flood prevention challenge and an upcoming blockchain based challenge.

Unleash Your Geek 1.0

We believe San José’s people are our greatest asset. Unleash Your Geek challenges the community to create technology solutions to government challenges.

Unleash Your Geek 2.0

After the February 2017 floods, we sent out a call for innovation towards a more responsive and accurate flood detection system. Student teams from local universities are collaborating with emergency respondents to streamline the communication to keep our residents safe when they need it most.

Unleash Your Geek 3.0

The upcoming third installment of the Unleash Your Geek Civic Challenge will focus on innovative solutions to solve civic problems leveraging blockchain technologies. Potential use cases will leverage the promise of anonymity, security, and equal access of blockchain technologies, seeking solutions in digital identification, and civic engagement and outreach in budget and census efforts
